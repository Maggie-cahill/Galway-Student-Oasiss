Seven

Seven is an infamous cocktail bar which is right in the city
center. The cocktails taste amazing and the enviornment is
astouding. The light is not harsh and there's plenty of
comfortable booths to sit around with your friend's on a night out!
In our opinion it is one of the nicest bars to go to with your friends.


Busker Brownes

Busker Brownes is the main bar that everyone goes to.
It is packed almost every night as it is I think almost every
students favourite bar. It has good music, good drinks, and fun company.
If you want to meet lots of fun people and have an overall great night
this is the place to be.


The Kings Head

The Kings Head is a beautiful bar in Galway City. It has two
floors and live music. The seating is comfortable and there's
plenty of space, so bring as many friend's as you like!
It can get quite busy and the music is loud so if you are looking
for conversation it might not be the place for you. It is definetly
worth a visit though a there's no bad things to say about it!


An Púcán

An Púcán is a lovely and fancy bar right in the center of Galway City.
It has an outdoor/indoor smoking area with fake grass covering the floor.
It is a bit pricey so beware but the enviornment is beautiful and
definetly worth a visit!


The Merlin Bar

The Merlin Bar is a small bar that is just down the road
from the Atlantic University of Galway. It is a nice place to go
even though it is not the most popular. It is small and generally
filled with older company, but it is nice for a quiet few and a chill night.


Sult

If you go to college at or near the University of Galway
this is the perfect place to go! During the day it is a
lovely cafe but at the night time itturns into the place to be,
with a nice lay out, high ceilings, and a wide range of drinks
to chose from it is by far a great place to go to especially location
wise if you are looking to blow off steam with a few friends after a long day of uni.
