Black Cat

179 Upper Salthill, Salthill, Galway, Ireland

The Black cat is a lovely hotel in the center of Salthill.
The size of the accomodation brings a homey feel to it. This is
what the double room looks like, and as you can see even though
it is not much in size, it is still spectacular.

For an accomodation that is in the center of a very popular
location to visit, the price could not be any better,
Which is why it is first on our list to recommend to students.

Each room comes with a private bathroom, which is
fairly spacious. They are beautiful from the tiles to
the layout and use of space.

Menlo Park Hotel

Headford Road, Galway, Ireland

The Menlo Park Hotel is a beautiful hotel,
which is located only 2km outside of the city center
(6 minute drive or 28 minute walk)

It is a medium sized hotel which is made from
cobbelstone, which gives the hotel a beautiful atmosphere.

All rooms have a beautiful en-suite and some have
an absolutely stunning deep oval tub. The perfect place to relax
while on a vacation in Galway.
