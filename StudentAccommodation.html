Introduction


Housing can be a hassle for a student and finding the perfect location can be difficult to manage but through student villages, individuals can feel a sense of comfortability with their new found community. Each village offers different ammenities but all are student oriented and provide various advantages in comparison to other housing options. Below are some of the most popular and well known student villages within Galway. Take a look and consider the many possibilities that lie ahead of you this coming school year.

Glasan Village




Description

Glasan Village is a purpose built student accommodation complex, affiliated with ATU Galway. The Village comprises of 700 beds spaces, the majority of which are centrally managed through the onsite reception office, which has an adjacent security office, shop, restaurant and launderette. Its unrivalled location just across the road from the main ATU Galway Campus makes it the perfect choice for student accommodation in Galway.


Onsite Services

Some of the included ammenities and services include 24/7 security, 18 Cable Tv Channels, Managed WiFi Network, Onsite Maintenance Service (Mon - Fri), Soccer Court, Full Waste Management & Recycling Service, Entrance Gate, Car Parking (€10 Permits), and Bicycle Storage.

Dunares Village



Description

Dunares Village is a student oriented set of apartment complexes that offer a range of different living options and accommodations in Galway. Within walking distance of multiple restaurant establishments, local shops, and university campuses, Dunares is well positioned for any student. Some bedrooms are twin occupancy, some are single occupancy, some are doubles, which are rooms with a double bed, for single occupancy only. Apart from the size of room and the type of bed(s) all bedrooms have identical facilities.

Onsite Services

Dunaras Holiday Village offers modern self-catering apartments. The apartments feature bright living areas with leather sofas and a well-equipped, solid oak fitted kitchen with an oven/hob, microwave, and fridge. Other facilitie include an offered TV, Managed WiFi Network, Onsite Security, Launderette, Soccer Court, Recycling Service, Bicycle Storage, Car Parking, and CCTV.

Corrib Village




Description

Corrib Village provides comfortable and secure accommodation for students in the heart of student residences on the University campus, and accommodates 764 students in 176 non-smoking/vaping apartments.Apartments are traditional in style and each contains 3 or 4 bedrooms and a shared kitchen and living area. Corrib Village affords the greatest selection of bedroom options; twin, single and double bedrooms are available. Standard rooms come with a shared bathroom. En suite room options are also available.

Onsite Services

Corrib Village remains within walking distance of classes and university amenities and features access to a complimentary social events programme, on-site self-service launderette, convenience shop/café, bicycle storage, tennis court, a common room, 24/7 support on site, and a football cage.
